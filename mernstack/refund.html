We aim to ensure customer satisfaction. Please read our return and refund policy carefully.

1. Return Eligibility

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are damaged, defective, or incorrect. The item must be unused and in its original packaging.We accept returns for eligible products within a specified period from the date of delivery. To qualify for a return, the item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by proof of purchase. Perishable goods, personal care items, and customized products may not be eligible for returns.

2. Refund Process

Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item has been inspected. Refunds will be credited to the original payment method. If your refund is delayed, please contact your bank or our support team for assistance.

3. Exchange Policy

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, you can request an exchange. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.For a smooth exchange process, please report the issue within 48 hours of receiving the product and ensure that it is returned in its original condition.

4. Non-Returnable Items

Certain products like perishable goods, personal care items, and customized orders are non-returnable.

  • Perishable goods such as fresh fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and frozen food.
  • Personal care and hygiene products like skincare, cosmetics, and health supplements.
  • Customized or personalized items made to order.
  • Gift cards and promotional vouchers.

To avoid inconvenience, please carefully check the product details and descriptions before making a purchase.

5. How to Initiate a Return

  1. To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@greenmart.com with your order details.
  2. Provide your order number, item details, reason for return, and clear images (if applicable).
  3. Our team will review your request and guide you on the return shipping process or pickup options.
  4. Once the item is received and verified, we will process your refund or exchange as per policy.
For any further questions or assistance, feel free to reach out to us. Your satisfaction is our priority!

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our return and refund policy, please reach out to our support team.
